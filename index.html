CRYPTOMONNAIES WHAT IS IT?

What We Do for You?

Having moved from a theoretical concept in 1998 to a veritable alternative financial system today, crypto-currencies have become increasingly important in recent years, particularly thanks to their storage and transmission technology. information, the "Blockchain" .

Crypto-currencies are Peer-to-Peer currencies and exist only through a global network of computers that allows them to operate. When a money transaction is issued from one individual to another, it is transmitted and validated by the computers that make up the network.

Discover and learn what are Bitcoin, crypto-currencies and blockchain on Cryptoast.fr .

WHAT IS UNICEF 'S APPROACH ?

Today, we always take the same people with the same methods, but cryptocurrencies and their revolutionary approach are an opportunity to raise funds differently .
Thanks to the solidarity of the community of PC players, we conducted a first operation named Game Chaingers which made it possible to turn graphics cards into a humanitarian tool . This was Ethereum's first fundraising fundraiser for a charity.

WHICH CRYPTOMONNAIES ARE ACCEPTED?

Bitcoin , Ethereum , DAI ,Litecoin ,Ripple , Monero, EOS and Stellar.

DO I HAVE A TAX RECEIPT?

No, because donations in cryptocurrency are not the subject of tax deduction.

HOW TO PROTECT ?

Phishers can send you or post a website that looks like UNICEF France , but it's not the real website. It is important to have some security checks. These will be detailed on the donation page .
